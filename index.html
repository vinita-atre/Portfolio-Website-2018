About Me

Currently, I'm an HCI master's candidate at the University of Maryland. I am also working as the lead UX Researcher for my Capstone project Park Pal with the Montgomery County Park Services.

After an undergrad in CS, I worked as a Software Engineer for an e-commerce company, Infibeam. There I collaborated with the product manager and UX designer to co-design and develop functional prototypes and proof of concept B2B and B2C mobile apps. I also designed and implemented Google Analytics for understanding user activity along with unit testing and usability review. Passionate to solve user problems through design, I joined the HCIM program.

I am self starter who uses design driven by research and data to solve problems for users and businesses. To increase awareness about user profiling and content targetting, I created a personality quiz at the innovtationWorksLab for CNN health.

I'm open to full-time opportunities starting July 2018! If you're interested in any of my works, please contact me at vinitalatre@gmail.com

My Design Process

Discover

Contextual inquiry, User interview, Field research, Survey, Brainstorming, Data Analytics, Literature review, Participatory design, Storyboarding, Scenario Mapping,

Define

Problem definition - User goal, JTBD and business goal, Persona, Affinity diagrams, Experience Models - Day in Life Model, Identity Model and Empathy Map, User flows, Visioning, User stories, User Environment Design, MVP design, Design Workshops

Prototype

Iterative Design, Sketching, Wireflows, Wireframes,Copy writing, Information Architecture, High fidelity prototype, functional prototypes, technical proof of concept, Visual design

Test

Heuristic evaluation, Cognitive walkthrough, Usability testing - quantitative and qualitative, User studies, Contextual design, Accessibility review, Voice UX review, User flow testing, Unit testing, Statistical Analysis
